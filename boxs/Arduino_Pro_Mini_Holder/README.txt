# Summary

This is a simple holder for an Arduino Pro Mini.  The picture tells the story.

Normally, a pro mini would be secured via the header pins but what if you wanted to anchored it with screws?   Well, late at night, I decided I would have a go at designing a simple holder and much to my surprise, it tested perfect.  
The fit is precise, the pins are not obstructed and the board is held very secure.
